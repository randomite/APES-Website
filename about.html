About Us

At Student CAP Initiative, we are committed to bringing individuals together to promote a sustainable, healthy planet one small step at a time. Originally a group of Environmental Science students wishing to reduce greenhouse gas emissions in our hometown of Sunnyvale, California, we decided to band together to promote two major goals: 1. partner with the City of Sunnyvale to pass the local Climate Action Plan and 2. educate the public on how to become environmental advocates within their spheres of influence (neighborhoods, communities, schools, and workplaces). Ultimately, we wish to empower individuals, students and adults alike, and create programs that serve as models to be emulated throughout the nation. Please feel free to contribute to our endeavors by coming to the student council meeting on May 20th at the Sunnyvale City Council meeting site, whereupon council members will vote on the proposed legislation!
