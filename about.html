About Us

At (project name), we are committed to exposing the current water crisis to the public and bringing individuals together to promote a healthy planet, one small step at a time. Originally a group of Environmental Science students wishing to promote change in the inefficient water system used in our hometown of Cupertino, California, we decided to band together to promote two major goals: 1. Implement rainwater catchment systems to reduce water wastage and 2. Educate the public on how to become effecting environmental neighborhoods in their spheres of influence (neighborhoods, communities, schools, and workplaces). Ultimately, we wish to empower individuals, students and adults alike, and create programs that serve as models to be emulated throughout the nation.
